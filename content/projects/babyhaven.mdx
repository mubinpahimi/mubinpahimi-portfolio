# Babyhaven.my – UX & Social Media Design
**Case study**  
Published on September, 2021 · 5 min read  

I worked as a UX Designer & Social Media Designer for Babyhaven.my, a Malaysia-based online store specializing in baby products. The project focused on improving website UX and creating a social media design system to engage parents and drive traffic.  

[View Project](https://babyhaven.my)

---

## Overview
Babyhaven.my offers infant essentials such as clothing, gear, and accessories. By 2021, the brand had an active Instagram presence (@babyhavenmy), sharing product updates, parenting tips, quotes, and customer reviews. The goal was to make browsing and purchasing seamless, while reinforcing the brand across digital touchpoints.  

## Role
**UX Designer & Social Media Designer** – Responsibilities included:  
- Conducting user research to understand Malaysian parents’ shopping behaviors and pain points  
- Designing information architecture (IA) and mobile-first wireframes  
- Creating high-fidelity UI mockups focusing on usability, accessibility, and visual hierarchy  
- Developing Instagram content templates aligned with the website UX and brand tone  
- Conducting usability testing and iterating designs based on feedback  

## Project Objective
- Improve website UX for seamless browsing and purchasing of baby products  
- Develop a social media design system to reinforce the brand and engage the target audience  

## UX Process

### 1. Research & User Insights
- Surveys and interviews with parents to identify pain points in online shopping  
- Competitor analysis of Malaysian baby e-commerce platforms  

**Key Insights:**  
- Users want clear product categorization and easy filtering  
- Mobile experience is critical—most parents browse on phones  
- Parents rely heavily on reviews and product details before purchasing  

### 2. Information Architecture & Wireframes
- Reorganized product categories for quick scanning and navigation  
- Introduced filter and sort functions for ease of product discovery  
- Designed consistent layout patterns to reduce cognitive load  

### 3. Visual & Interaction Design
- Developed visual hierarchy highlighting product images, prices, and reviews  
- Designed interactive elements like hover states and sticky CTAs  
- Applied a friendly, trustworthy aesthetic aligned with parenting and baby themes  

### 4. Usability Testing & Iteration
- Conducted remote usability testing with 10+ parents  
- Observed behaviors like difficulty in filtering products and viewing reviews  
- Iterated designs: improved filter visibility, emphasized ratings, optimized checkout flow  

### 5. Social Media Design
- Created Instagram templates that complement website UX  
- Focused on educational and engaging content (parenting tips, product highlights) guiding users to the website  

---

## Key Outcomes
- Improved product discoverability and clarity through website redesign  
- Mobile-first design led to smoother browsing for on-the-go parents  
- Reduced friction in navigation and checkout via usability testing insights  
- Social media design supported website UX by driving traffic and engagement  
- Established a scalable UX and visual system for future updates

## Reflection
This project combined **user-centered design principles** with **visual branding** to enhance Babyhaven.my’s digital presence. By focusing on UX, we reduced user friction, improved mobile experiences, and created a cohesive journey across the website and social media channels, strengthening trust and engagement with Malaysian parents.
